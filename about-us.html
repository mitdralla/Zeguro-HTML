    About Us

    With simplicity at its core, Zeguro enables you to automate your cybersecurity processes, detect for any cyber risks in your people, process and technology, and provide ongoing risk recommendations to reduce your overall cyber risk. Hackers and cyber attackers never rest – if they succeed, Zeguro’s innovative and tailored insurance will help your business recover from such interruptions. Zeguro is the world’s first truly tech-enabled insurance product.
